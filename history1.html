History of In-N-Out

History

In 1948, Harry created the first two-way speaker box to start the trend of Drive-Thrus.

In the 1960s, In-N-Out began offering their customers their "secret menu", consisting of beloved items such as animal fries, 3x3, 4x4, and many more options.

In 1976, Harry Snyder, the founder of In-N-Out passed away, leaving his wife, Esther Snyder, to lead the company to grow significantly.
